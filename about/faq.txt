1. What is CognitoAttend?
CognitoAttend is a smart digital attendance management system designed to track presence accurately using QR-based check-ins, real-time monitoring, and automated reports.

2. How does QR attendance work?
An admin generates a time-limited QR code. Users scan it using their device, enter basic details, and their attendance is marked instantly. The QR refreshes automatically to prevent misuse.

3. Can students or users fake attendance?
No. CognitoAttend uses dynamic QR refresh, session validation, and optional location/device checks to minimize proxy or duplicate attendance.
